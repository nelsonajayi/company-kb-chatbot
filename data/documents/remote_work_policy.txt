ACME CORPORATION - REMOTE WORK POLICY

Effective Date: March 1, 2024

1. ELIGIBILITY
All employees who have completed their probationary period (90 days) are eligible for remote work.
Contractors and interns are not eligible for remote work.

2. REMOTE WORK SCHEDULE
- Employees may work remotely up to 3 days per week
- Must be in office on Tuesdays and Thursdays (core collaboration days)
- Remote days must be consistent each week and approved by manager
- Schedule changes require 2 weeks notice

3. EQUIPMENT PROVIDED
The company provides the following equipment for remote work:
- Laptop computer (Dell or MacBook)
- External monitor (up to 27 inches)
- Keyboard and mouse
- Headset with microphone
- $500 one-time home office stipend for desk, chair, etc.

4. HOME OFFICE REQUIREMENTS
- Dedicated workspace with reliable internet (minimum 50 Mbps)
- Quiet environment suitable for video calls
- Ergonomic setup to prevent injury

5. EXPECTATIONS AND AVAILABILITY
- Must be available during core hours (10am-3pm EST)
- Must attend all scheduled meetings via video
- Response time to Slack messages: within 2 hours during work hours
- Same productivity standards as in-office work

6. SECURITY
- Use company VPN when accessing internal systems
- Do not work from public WiFi networks
- Lock computer when away from desk
- No family members should access company equipment
