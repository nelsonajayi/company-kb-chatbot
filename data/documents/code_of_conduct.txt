ACME CORPORATION - CODE OF CONDUCT

Our Commitment: ACME is committed to maintaining a respectful, inclusive, and ethical workplace.

1. PROFESSIONAL BEHAVIOR
- Treat all colleagues, clients, and partners with respect
- Communicate professionally in all channels (email, Slack, meetings)
- Dress appropriately for your role (business casual minimum for client meetings)
- Be punctual for meetings and commitments

2. DIVERSITY AND INCLUSION
- Zero tolerance for discrimination based on race, gender, age, religion, disability, or sexual orientation
- Respect different perspectives and backgrounds
- Use inclusive language
- Report any discrimination immediately to HR

3. HARASSMENT POLICY
- No form of harassment will be tolerated
- This includes verbal, physical, or visual harassment
- Sexual harassment is strictly prohibited
- Retaliation against those who report harassment is prohibited

4. CONFLICTS OF INTEREST
- Disclose any potential conflicts to your manager
- Do not use company resources for personal gain
- Outside employment requires approval
- Do not accept gifts over $50 from vendors

5. CONFIDENTIALITY
- Protect company confidential information
- Do not share proprietary information with competitors
- Client information is strictly confidential
- Sign NDA when required

6. SOCIAL MEDIA
- Do not speak on behalf of the company without authorization
- Use good judgment when posting about work
- Do not share confidential information
- Be respectful when discussing the company online

7. REPORTING VIOLATIONS
- Report violations to your manager or HR
- Anonymous ethics hotline: 1-800-XXX-XXXX
- No retaliation for good faith reports
- Violations may result in termination
