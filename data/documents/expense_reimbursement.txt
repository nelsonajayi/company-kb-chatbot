ACME CORPORATION - EXPENSE REIMBURSEMENT POLICY

Last Updated: February 2024

1. ELIGIBLE EXPENSES
The following business expenses are reimbursable when properly documented:

TRAVEL EXPENSES:
- Airfare (economy class only, business class requires VP approval)
- Hotels (up to $200/night, higher rates need pre-approval)
- Rental cars (mid-size or smaller)
- Parking and tolls
- Mileage (current IRS rate: $0.67 per mile)

MEALS:
- Breakfast: up to $15
- Lunch: up to $25
- Dinner: up to $35
- Daily maximum: $75
- Must be during business travel (excludes daily commute)

CLIENT ENTERTAINMENT:
- Requires pre-approval from manager
- Maximum $100 per person
- Must include business purpose and attendees

PROFESSIONAL DEVELOPMENT:
- Conferences and training courses (pre-approved)
- Professional certifications
- Books and online courses (up to $500/year)

2. SUBMISSION PROCESS
- Submit expenses within 30 days of incurring them
- Use Expensify mobile app
- Attach photos of all receipts
- Include detailed business purpose
- Manager approval required for expenses over $500
- CFO approval required for expenses over $2,000

3. NON-REIMBURSABLE EXPENSES
- Alcoholic beverages (except client entertainment)
- Personal entertainment (movies, spa, gym)
- Upgrades (car, hotel, flight) not required for business
- Traffic violations and parking tickets
- Personal phone calls

4. REIMBURSEMENT TIMELINE
- Approved expenses are reimbursed within 2 business days
- Payment via direct deposit to your bank account
- Check your Expensify dashboard for status

5. CORPORATE CREDIT CARDS
- Available to directors and above
- Must reconcile monthly
- Personal charges are prohibited
- Lost cards must be reported immediately
